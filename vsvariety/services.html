SERVICES

V&S Variety strives to provide the products and supplies you need at an affordable price. From crafts to paint, plumbing to toys, we are certain you will find what you need within our aisles.

HARDWARE:

Start and finish your home improvement project here. We offer a large selection of hand and power tools, electrical and plumbing supplies, a full line of light bulbs, caulking, sealing, and a rainbow of paints and finishes to choose from. And of course plenty of duct tape!

CRAFTS & SEWING:

From fabric to thread, needles to yarn, we have it all. Everything you need for your arts and crafts project including paints, dyes, brushes, poster board, foam board, markers, and much more can be found in our crafts section.

HOUSEWARE:

A complete line of housewares. Small appliances, cutlery, glassware, pots, pans, and coolers. Also a full line of cleaning supplies including cleaners, brooms, mops, buckets, and vacuums.

LAWN & GARDEN:

Our seasonal department includes a full line of lawn & garden supplies to keep your grass green and your flowers growing. We have bags of soil, mulch, manure, and fertilizer outside. Inside we have garden tools, grass seeds, and seed and feed for the birds. In winter months, we stock snow shovels and ice melt to help with your snow removal chores. Power lawn & garden and winter items also available by special order.

WATCH REPAIR:

Let us bring life back to your favorite timepiece. Batteries of all sizes, straps of all types, sold and installed by our friendly staff.

CARDS & CANDLES:

Greeting cards for every occasion from Ambassador by Hallmark. We are also a registered Yankee Candle™ retailer with jar candles, votive candles, Scenterpiece™ warmers and scents.

SPORTING GOODS & TOYS:

We offer toys for all seasons for kids young and old. Small toys, Legos™, games, puzzles, cards, crayons, etc for every day. Balls, yard games, fishing supplies, pool toys, and more for seasonal fun.

PARTY BALLOONS:

Balloons, gifts, wrapping paper, and more! We have a large selection of party and special occasion balloons, as well as a variety of colors. All your party supplies in one place, and we even blow up your balloons with helium right in the store. Let us put together your balloon bouquet today!

SCHOOL & OFFICE SUPPLIES:

Notebooks, pens, envelopes, tapes, everything you need to stock your office, or to prepare kids for school days. We can also special order office furniture and equipment.

CLOTHING:

A large selection of clothing, from every-day wear to a special item commemorating your trip. T-shirts, sweatshirts, sweatpants, pull-overs, and hats available from Berg Active-ware another Maine owned company.

PRINTER INK:

We now stock a wide array of printer ink, and can special order many others. Let our friendly staff know the make and model of your printer, and we'll find the refill you need with a delivery time of usually one or two days if we have to order it for you.
